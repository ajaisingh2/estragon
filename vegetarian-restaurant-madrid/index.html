El Estragón, vegetarian restaurant in Madrid

If you wish to enjoy the best Lacto-Ovo Vegetarian food, come to our Vegetarian Restaurant El Estragón, located in Plaza de la Paja, in the old part of town, (the Austria's Madrid historical quarter).

El Estragón is an old tavern converted into a Vegetarian Restaurant, where you can enjoy a wide selection of delicious and tasty vegetarian dishes together with your non vegetarian friends.
